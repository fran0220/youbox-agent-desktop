# travel-destination

Scenic travel poster evoking wanderlust and the romance of exploration.

## Purpose
Tourism promotion, travel agency campaigns, wanderlust content, airline and hospitality marketing.

## Composition
- **Hero zone**: Scenic landscape or landmark as full-bleed hero image
- **Info zone**: Destination name overlaid on scenery with strong contrast
- **Action zone**: Travel details optional, vintage travel poster layout encouraged

## Visual Elements
- Iconic landmarks and architectural silhouettes
- Natural scenery: mountains, coastlines, skylines
- Cultural elements: local color, traditional patterns
- Warm golden-hour lighting and inviting horizon lines

## Text Placement
- **Destination name**: Bold and decorative — often serif or display typeface with character
- **Tagline/slogan**: Evocative one-liner below destination name
- **Travel details**: Dates, pricing, or agency info — optional and understated
- **CTA**: Minimal, often just a website or QR code

## Recommended Pairings
- `vintage-retro`: Classic travel poster aesthetic, timeless appeal
- `art-deco`: Luxury travel and premium destinations
- `storybook-watercolor`: Romantic, painterly, and dreamlike quality
- `craft-handmade`: Adventure travel and off-the-beaten-path destinations
