# motivational

Inspirational quote poster where words carry the emotional weight.

## Purpose
Inspirational quotes, office and workspace decor, social media content, personal development branding.

## Composition
- **Hero zone**: Powerful background image at 100% bleed — image serves the text
- **Info zone**: Quote text overlaid with high contrast, centered or rule-of-thirds
- **Action zone**: Minimal — author attribution only

## Visual Elements
- Epic landscapes: mountain peaks, ocean horizons, sunrise/sunset
- Single person silhouette for scale and aspiration
- Dramatic sky with cloud formations or star fields
- Minimalist composition — fewer elements, stronger impact

## Text Placement
- **Quote**: Hero element — largest text on canvas, high-contrast overlay
- **Author attribution**: Small, understated, positioned below quote
- **Other text**: Minimal to none — let the words breathe
- **Branding**: Optional watermark or small logo, corner-anchored

## Recommended Pairings
- `swiss-typography`: Clean, modern, and authoritative
- `aged-academia`: Classic wisdom and scholarly gravitas
- `brutalist`: Bold, confrontational impact
- `storybook-watercolor`: Gentle, reflective, and nurturing tone
