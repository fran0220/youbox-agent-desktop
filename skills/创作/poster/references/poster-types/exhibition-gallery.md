# exhibition-gallery

Exhibition and gallery poster — sophisticated presentation for art world events.

## Purpose
Art exhibitions, museum shows, gallery openings, retrospectives, and curated collections.

## Composition
- **Hero zone** (50–60%): Featured artwork or elegant design as focal point
- **Info zone** (25–30%): Generous whitespace with sophisticated typography
- **Action zone** (15%): Opening details and venue information

## Visual Elements
- Featured artwork reproduction at gallery quality
- Gallery-quality framing and presentation
- Minimal decoration — let the art speak
- Elegant negative space as a design element
- Refined details and subtle finishing touches

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Exhibition title — elegant and prominent
- **Subtitle**: Artist name in refined typography
- **Body**: Venue, dates, and curatorial note
- **CTA**: Opening reception details
- **Footer**: Gallery logo, address, hours

## Recommended Pairings
- `art-deco`: classic and timeless presentation
- `swiss-typography`: modern gallery aesthetic
- `aged-academia`: historical exhibitions and retrospectives
- `brutalist`: contemporary and avant-garde art
