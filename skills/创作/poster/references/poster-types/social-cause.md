# social-cause

Social cause and awareness campaign poster — designed to move hearts and mobilize action.

## Purpose
Environmental campaigns, humanitarian appeals, social justice movements, nonprofit awareness drives, and public health messaging.

## Composition
- **Hero zone** (60%): Emotionally powerful image dominates, message overlaid with impact
- **Info zone** (25%): Supporting evidence, statistics, context
- **Action zone** (15%): Clear call-to-action — donate, sign, share, act

## Visual Elements
- Powerful photography or illustration as centerpiece
- Emotional subjects — wildlife, people, nature, communities
- Symbolic imagery reinforcing the cause
- Limited color palette for maximum impact
- High contrast between hope and urgency

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Campaign message — bold, direct, impossible to ignore
- **Subtitle**: Statistics or facts as supporting evidence
- **Body**: Brief context or personal story
- **CTA**: Action items (donate / sign / share) — prominent and unmissable
- **Footer**: Organization logo and website

## Recommended Pairings
- `storybook-watercolor`: empathy and emotional connection
- `swiss-typography`: authority and credibility
- `bold-graphic`: urgency and immediacy
- `craft-handmade`: grassroots authenticity
