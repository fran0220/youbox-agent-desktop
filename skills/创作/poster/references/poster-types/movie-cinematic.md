# movie-cinematic

Cinematic movie poster designed for maximum dramatic impact and star power.

## Purpose
Theater promotion, streaming platform key art, film festival submissions, home media packaging.

## Composition
- **Hero zone**: Character portrait or key scene dominates 60-70% of canvas
- **Info zone**: Title in upper or lower third with stylized treatment
- **Action zone**: Credits block anchored at bottom, release date and rating

## Visual Elements
- Dramatic lighting with strong directional sources
- Character silhouettes and heroic poses
- Atmospheric effects: fog, particles, volumetric light beams
- Cinematic color grading (teal-orange, desaturated, monochrome wash)

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Large, bold, stylized treatment — often custom lettering or heavily modified typeface
- **Tagline**: Centered above or below title, smaller italic or condensed weight
- **Credits block**: Industry-standard Univers-style condensed at bottom
- **Rating/release date**: Bottom corners or integrated with credits

## Recommended Pairings
- `film-noir`: Perfect for thriller, drama, and mystery genres
- `cyberpunk-neon`: Sci-fi and futuristic narratives
- `vintage-retro`: Period pieces and nostalgic storytelling
- `bold-graphic`: High-octane action and superhero films
