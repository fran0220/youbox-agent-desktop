# product-launch

Premium product reveal poster designed for anticipation and desire.

## Purpose
New product announcements, tech reveals, fashion releases, brand campaigns, pre-order promotions.

## Composition
- **Hero zone**: Product floating center with dramatic lighting, occupies 50-60%
- **Info zone**: Clean background with ample negative space, text anchored top and bottom
- **Action zone**: Launch date, CTA, and brand logo at bottom

## Visual Elements
- Product hero shot with studio-quality lighting
- Light trails and subtle particle effects
- Reflective surfaces and premium material textures
- Gradient backgrounds or minimal environmental context

## Text Placement
- **Product name**: Large, modern sans-serif — clean and authoritative
- **Tagline**: One-liner positioned near product, medium weight
- **Key specs/features**: Concise bullet points or single-line highlights
- **Launch date + brand logo**: Bottom anchored, balanced composition

## Recommended Pairings
- `swiss-typography`: Premium, clean, and high-end positioning
- `brutalist`: Bold statement launches and disruptive products
- `corporate-memphis`: Friendly, approachable consumer products
- `technical-schematic`: Engineering-forward and spec-driven reveals
