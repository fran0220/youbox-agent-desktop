# food-restaurant

Food and restaurant poster — warm, inviting, and appetite-inducing.

## Purpose
Restaurant promotion, food festival marketing, recipe showcase, menu specials, and culinary event announcements.

## Composition
- **Hero zone** (60%): Close-up hero food shot, warm and inviting atmosphere
- **Info zone** (25%): Text integrated naturally with the scene
- **Action zone** (15%): Offer details, location, and hours

## Visual Elements
- Beautifully plated food as the centerpiece
- Fresh ingredients visible — herbs, produce, garnishes
- Warm lighting with steam and texture details
- Rustic surfaces — wood, stone, linen
- Kitchen ambiance and human touch

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Dish or restaurant name — appetizing and warm
- **Subtitle**: Description of flavors and ingredients
- **Body**: Price or special offer details
- **CTA**: Location, hours, reservation info
- **Footer**: Social media handles, delivery options

## Recommended Pairings
- `craft-handmade`: artisan and farm-to-table feel
- `morandi-journal`: warm muted tones, editorial quality
- `vintage-retro`: classic diner and nostalgic charm
- `kawaii`: cute cafe and dessert shop aesthetic
