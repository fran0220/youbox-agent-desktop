# music-album

Music album cover poster — artistic, conceptual, and mood-driven.

## Purpose
Album covers, EP artwork, single release visuals, and playlist covers.

## Composition
- **Hero zone** (85–95%): Artistic visual fills entire space, highly conceptual
- **Info zone** (5–10%): Minimal text, integrated into design
- **Action zone** (minimal): Release info only when needed

## Visual Elements
- Abstract or symbolic imagery as the full canvas
- Emotional atmosphere driving the entire composition
- Artistic photography or conceptual illustration
- Mood-driven color palette reflecting the music
- Texture and grain for tactile quality

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Artist name and album title only
- **Subtitle**: Minimal — sometimes omitted entirely
- **Body**: None — the visual is the message
- **CTA**: Hidden or stylized into the artwork
- **Footer**: Record label logo, parental advisory if needed

## Recommended Pairings
- `film-noir`: dark, moody, and cinematic
- `art-deco`: elegant and timeless
- `pixel-art`: electronic and chiptune genres
- `cyberpunk-neon`: synth, vaporwave, and future bass
