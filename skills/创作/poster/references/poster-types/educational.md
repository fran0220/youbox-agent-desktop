# educational

Educational and knowledge poster — clear, structured, and approachable.

## Purpose
Classroom materials, course promotion, knowledge infographics, science communication, and learning aids.

## Composition
- **Hero zone** (60%): Structured layout with sections, illustrations supporting text
- **Info zone** (30%): Clear visual hierarchy with digestible chunks
- **Action zone** (10%): Source credits and further reading

## Visual Elements
- Diagrams, icons, and illustrations
- Charts and data visualizations
- Numbered steps and process flows
- Visual metaphors for abstract concepts
- Friendly, approachable graphics

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Topic title — clear and engaging
- **Subtitle**: Key concepts as section headings
- **Body**: Brief explanations, fun facts, or statistics
- **CTA**: "Learn more" or course enrollment info
- **Footer**: Source credits and references

## Recommended Pairings
- `corporate-memphis`: modern education and online courses
- `craft-handmade`: kids-friendly and elementary level
- `chalkboard`: classroom nostalgia and academic feel
- `kawaii`: playful learning and young audience engagement
