# sports-event

Dynamic sports event poster radiating competitive intensity and athletic power.

## Purpose
Championships, matches, tournaments, fitness events, marathon promotions, esports competitions.

## Composition
- **Hero zone**: Action shot of athlete in motion, ~60% of canvas
- **Info zone**: Event name and dynamic energy framing the athlete
- **Action zone**: Date, venue, team names, and championship branding at bottom

## Visual Elements
- Motion blur and freeze-frame action captures
- Explosive energy effects: sparks, shattered elements, speed lines
- Stadium atmosphere with lighting and crowd depth
- Dynamic camera angles: low-angle power shots, diagonal compositions
- National colors or team palettes as accent

## Text Placement
- **Event name**: Bold, aggressive typeface — often italicized or slanted for speed
- **Date + venue**: Clear and prominent secondary line
- **Team names**: Versus layout or side-by-side when applicable
- **Championship branding**: Logo and series identity, top or bottom anchored

## Recommended Pairings
- `bold-graphic`: High-energy mainstream sports
- `brutalist`: Raw power and combat sports
- `cyberpunk-neon`: Esports and gaming tournaments
- `corporate-memphis`: Corporate-sponsored sporting events
