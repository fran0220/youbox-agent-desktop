# event-concert

High-energy concert and live event poster built for excitement and urgency.

## Purpose
Concerts, music festivals, live events, club nights, parties, and touring promotions.

## Composition
- **Hero zone**: Performer photo or stage visual occupies ~50%, layered with depth
- **Info zone**: Event name and artist dominate upper portion, date and venue prominent
- **Action zone**: Ticket info, sponsors, and additional acts at bottom

## Visual Elements
- Stage lighting with colored gels and spotlights
- Crowd silhouettes adding scale and energy
- Energy effects: laser beams, lens flares, bokeh
- Dynamic motion blur and radial bursts

## Text Placement
- **Title**: Artist or event name — huge, bold, often filling full width
- **Date + venue**: Prominent secondary hierarchy, clear and scannable
- **Ticket info**: Price, on-sale date, website — action-oriented placement
- **Sponsors**: Logos row at bottom, small and uniform

## Recommended Pairings
- `bold-graphic`: Rock, punk, and high-energy genres
- `cyberpunk-neon`: Electronic, EDM, and synthwave events
- `vintage-retro`: Jazz, blues, and revival shows
- `craft-handmade`: Indie, folk, and intimate acoustic events
