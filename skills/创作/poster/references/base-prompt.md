## Core Poster Principles

- Text is the #1 priority — all text MUST be clearly legible and correctly spelled
- Maintain strong visual hierarchy: Title > Subtitle > Body > Details
- Leave safe margins (minimum 5% on each edge) for key content
- Use bold, high-contrast typography for the main title
- Keep total text under 50 words — every word earns its space
- Background should support, never compete with, foreground content

## Text Requirements

- **Title**: Large, bold, immediately readable from a distance. Positioned in the hero zone.
- **Subtitle**: Smaller but still prominent. Supports the title message.
- **Body/Details**: Concise, well-spaced. Date, location, or key facts.
- **CTA**: Clear call-to-action or event details at the bottom.
- All text in {{LANGUAGE}}. Render text accurately with no spelling errors.
