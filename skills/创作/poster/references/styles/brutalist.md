# brutalist

Raw, aggressive typography with unpolished industrial energy.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Raw concrete grey (#808080), off-white (#F0EDE5)
- Secondary: Deep black (#111111)
- Accents: Warning red (#FF3333), construction yellow (#FFD600), electric blue (#0066FF)
- Textures: Concrete, raw paper, rust, exposed material

## Visual Elements

- Oversized typography that breaks boundaries
- Deliberate "undesigned" aesthetic
- Overlapping elements and broken grids
- Raw textures: concrete, paper, metal
- Harsh contrasts and stark compositions
- Visible structure and construction
- Anti-decorative — function over form
- Collage-like layering

## Typography

- Extremely bold, condensed, heavy sans-serif
- Massive font sizes filling entire poster
- Mixed weights in same composition
- Monospaced or typewriter fonts for body
- Text as graphic element — rotated, stacked, cropped
- Intentionally "ugly" but impactful

## Best For

Tech conferences, indie music, avant-garde art, counter-culture events, startup launches, developer culture, underground scenes
