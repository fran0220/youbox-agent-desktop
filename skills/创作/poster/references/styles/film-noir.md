# film-noir

High contrast black and white with dramatic shadows and moody atmosphere.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Pure black (#000000), deep charcoal (#1a1a1a)
- Secondary: Silver grey (#C0C0C0), smoke white (#F5F5F5)
- Accents: Single accent color — blood red (#8B0000) or amber (#FFB000) used sparingly
- Gradients: Deep shadow gradients from black to dark grey

## Visual Elements

- High contrast chiaroscuro lighting
- Deep dramatic shadows with sharp edges
- Film grain and noise texture overlay
- Venetian blind shadow patterns
- Smoke, fog, and atmospheric haze
- Silhouettes and partial face reveals
- Rain-slicked surfaces and reflections
- Vintage film strip borders (optional)

## Typography

- Tall, condensed serif fonts for titles (Bodoni, Didot style)
- Art deco-influenced letterforms
- White or silver text on dark backgrounds
- Sometimes reversed — black text on white spotlight area
- Italic for taglines and subtitles

## Best For

Thriller, crime, drama films, detective stories, noir photography, mystery events, dark moody aesthetics
