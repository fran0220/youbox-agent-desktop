# art-deco

Luxurious geometric symmetry with gold accents and 1920s elegance.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Deep navy (#0D1B2A), emerald green (#064E3B), burgundy (#722F37)
- Secondary: Cream (#FFFDD0), ivory (#FFFFF0)
- Accents: Gold (#D4AF37), champagne (#F7E7CE), bronze (#CD7F32)
- Metallic finishes: gold foil, brass, copper effects

## Visual Elements

- Geometric symmetry and mirror patterns
- Sunburst and fan motifs
- Chevrons, zigzags, and stepped patterns
- Ornamental borders and corner decorations
- Egyptian and Aztec-influenced geometric forms
- Stylized figurative elements (eagles, women, fountains)
- Vertical lines emphasizing height and grandeur
- Gilt framing and decorative dividers

## Typography

- Geometric display fonts with sharp angles
- Art deco typefaces (Broadway, Bifur, Parisian style)
- All-caps titles with wide letter-spacing
- Thin elegant serifs for body text
- Decorative initial capitals
- Gold or cream colored text

## Best For

Luxury events, gatsby-themed parties, high-end brands, architectural exhibitions, fashion shows, awards ceremonies, cocktail bars
